About me

I have been passionate about computer science and electronics since childhood. I owe everything to my older cousin who was an electrician. However, I am moving more towards computer science, and more specifically, I want to become a programmer. I completed my first studies according to my preferences. Now, I'm halfway to getting my Master's degree in Business Analyst. I chose this direction to learn more about finance and how business works. After graduating from these studies, I would like to finish my master's degree in IT.

Traveling is my greatest hobby, I love visiting new places and learning about new customs and cultures. These are usually low-budget backpacking trips. Thailand was one of the most distant places I have visited so far. Below is a photo from Cheow Lan Lake. Thanks to this, I also get to know new places, I have already been on the slopes in Poland, but also in Italy and France.
